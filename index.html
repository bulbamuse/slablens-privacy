Collection of Information

The information we collect about you depends on how you interact with our Services. In this section, we describe the categories of information we collect and the sources of this information.

Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect the following information when you use our Services:

  • Web History: When you use SlabLens to analyze a trading card listing on eBay, we collect the URL of the eBay listing page you are viewing, the card title from that listing, and the timestamp of when you performed the analysis. This information is stored on our servers to prevent duplicate analyses of the same listing and to provide you with usage statistics.
  • Website Content: We collect card images, titles, and prices from eBay listing pages when you click the SlabLens extension icon to analyze a card. These images and data are sent to our backend service and to OpenAI's API for AI-powered condition analysis. Card images may be temporarily stored on our servers during the analysis process.
  • Anonymous User Identifier: We generate and store a unique anonymous identifier locally on your device (using Chrome's storage API). This identifier is sent to our backend service to track your usage count and enforce daily usage limits. This identifier does not contain any personally identifiable information and cannot be used to identify you.
  • Usage Statistics: We collect information about your usage of SlabLens, including the number of analyses you have performed, the dates and times of those analyses, and your daily usage patterns over the past 7 days. This data is stored on our servers in association with your anonymous identifier.
  • Analysis Results: We store the results of each card grading analysis, including the grade (1-10 scale), confidence level, and detailed condition assessment. This data is stored in association with the eBay listing URL and your anonymous identifier.

Information We Do Not Collect

SlabLens does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or other personally identifiable information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Authentication credentials or passwords
  • Location data, IP addresses, or GPS coordinates
  • Personal communications such as emails, texts, or chat messages
  • Browsing history outside of eBay trading card listings you choose to analyze

Use of Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our AI-powered card grading service
  • Analyze card condition and generate grading results using AI technology
  • Cache analysis results to avoid re-analyzing the same eBay listings
  • Track and enforce daily usage limits
  • Provide you with usage statistics and analysis history
  • Monitor and analyze trends and usage patterns to improve our Services
  • Detect, investigate, and help protect against security incidents and fraudulent activity
  • Comply with our legal and financial obligations

Disclosure of Information

We share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

  • OpenAI: We send card images extracted from eBay listings to OpenAI's API for AI-powered condition analysis. OpenAI processes these images according to their privacy policy and API terms of service. We do not send any personally identifiable information to OpenAI.
  • Supabase: We use Supabase as our backend database service to store anonymous user identifiers, usage statistics, analysis results, and eBay listing URLs. Supabase acts as our data processor and handles this information in accordance with their privacy policy.

eBay and Affiliate Partners

  • eBay Partner Network: When you open SlabLens on an eBay listing page, we automatically redirect you to an affiliate version of that listing URL. This redirect includes your anonymous identifier as a custom tracking parameter. If you make a purchase on eBay after this redirect, eBay may share transaction information with us through the eBay Partner Network, and we may earn a commission. See the Affiliate Links and Revenue section below for more details.

Aggregated or De-Identified Information

Affiliate Links and Revenue

Important Notice: SlabLens uses affiliate links to generate revenue.

When you open the SlabLens extension popup on an eBay listing page, we automatically redirect you to an affiliate version of that listing URL through the eBay Partner Network. This happens before you interact with the extension and does not require any action on your part.

The affiliate URL includes tracking parameters that identify the click as coming from SlabLens, as well as your anonymous user identifier. If you make a purchase on eBay after this redirect, we may earn a commission from eBay. This commission helps us keep SlabLens free to use.

What this means for you:

  • The price you pay on eBay is not affected by our affiliate links
  • You are not charged any additional fees for using SlabLens
  • eBay pays us the commission, not you
  • Your anonymous identifier may be shared with eBay through the affiliate link

Data Retention

We retain your anonymous identifier and usage data for as long as you continue to use SlabLens. Card images are temporarily stored during the analysis process and may be retained for caching purposes to improve performance.

Analysis results (grades, condition assessments) and eBay listing URLs are retained indefinitely to provide you with analysis history and to prevent duplicate analyses of the same listings.

Your Choices

Uninstalling the Extension

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the SlabLens extension from your browser. To uninstall, go to your Chrome extensions page (chrome://extensions/), find SlabLens, and click "Remove."

Clearing Local Data

Your anonymous identifier is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. Uninstalling the extension will clear this local data. Note that analysis results and usage statistics stored on our servers will remain unless you contact us to request deletion.

Data Deletion Requests

If you would like us to delete your analysis history and usage data from our servers, please contact us at bulba.lomuse@gmail.com with your anonymous user identifier (you can find this in the extension settings or usage statistics view).

Opting Out of Affiliate Links

The automatic affiliate link redirect is a core part of SlabLens's revenue model and cannot currently be disabled while using the extension. If you do not wish to use affiliate links, you will need to uninstall SlabLens.
